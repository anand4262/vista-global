Criminal Law

We provide strong legal representation in criminal matters, from bail applications and anticipatory bail to trial and appellate proceedings. With our roots in criminal litigation, we bring a sharp, tactical edge to every case we handle.

Our civil practice covers property disputes, injunctions, recovery suits, partition suits, contractual disagreements, and more. We work closely with our clients to protect their rights and resolve disputes efficiently.
